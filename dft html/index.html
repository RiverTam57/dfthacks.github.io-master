DFTHACKS IS A HACKTRAIN INITIATIVE TO INNOVATE THE TRANSPORT INDUSTRY.

HackTrain is arguably the craziest and most memorable hackathon experiences on earth! A weekend-long adventure hacking whilst on moving trains! We invite a group of the best developers, designers and entrepreneurs from all around the world to come together to build mobile apps, websites, products and services to solve problems in the railway industry.


THIS MARCH, WE HAVE BEEN COMMISSIONED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORT TO RUN DFTHACKS!!

WE LOOK FORWARD TO HEARING FROM YOU SOON!

  • What is a hackathon?

    Hackathons are endurance events which challenge small teams to intensively build products in an extrememly short space of time.

  • Can I apply?

    We welcome alßl programmers, designers and entrepreneurs! But if you're really interested in innovating the transport industry then we would love for you to apply too!

  • What would I have to bring for the DfTHacks weekdn?

    Nothing but a laptop, clothes and toiletries for the weekend! And yourself!

  • Are there any travel reimbursements provided?

    Yes there is! For applicants traveling from inside or outside the UK, we provide up to £25/£50 for travel reimbursement respectively. Reimbursements are limited and are given on a first come first serve basis to applicants, so apply early!

  • What will I eat?

    We will provide you food from Friday's lunch to Sunday's lunch. If you have dietary restrictions, we'll try out best to accommodate them! Don't forget the abundance of snacks we'll be providing throughout the hackathon weekend!

  • Where will I sleep?

    On Friday night, you would be provided with hotel accommodation, but on the second night there will be none as the idea of a hackathon is to hack overnight for 48 hours! However, you can still rest when you need to!!
